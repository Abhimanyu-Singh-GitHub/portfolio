Electric Go Kart

I built an Electric Go Kart in the summer of 2019, I bought an old rusted go kart and sanded down the frame and welded the front wheel arms as they weren't reinforced. The go kart recieved a paint job, then I fitted it with a 2kW Brushless DC motor, and a 48V 12Ah battery system and controller. After completing this build, I realized that the battery technology for this specifi use case was not readily available for under a good amount money. The sealed lead acid batteries that I used were simply not able to discharge at a rate fast enough to give me the right to push the motor to its limit. After this I decided to fit a tuned 4 stroke lawnmower engine this gave me more top speed and a much larger range as well. I miss the electric motor's torque, but the high revs of the 212cc is a lot of fun around corners.
